Hello! I’m Yidi.

After graduating from UC Berkeley with training in ec​onomics and legal studies, Yidi discovered that her passion is in writing, creating, and visual storytelling.

Born in China and raised in both Zhengzhou and Seattle, she currently lives in New York City where she looks for opportunities to get more involved in what she loves, while exploring rock climbing in the Hudson Valley. She is great at research, possesses expert command of both the Mandarin and the English language, and always excited to take on new challenges.

In the past, she has helped give voice to her Berkeley community by initiating a social media project featuring interviews with hopes to transcend the glass ceilings on race and gender to become a culture-maker.

Check back with the blog for fun reads and new ideas!
